Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the fraud detection system work?
The system analyzes transaction patterns using machine learning and flags unusual activities based on predefined risk factors.
What happens if a transaction is marked as fraudulent?
If a transaction is flagged, the system sends an email notification and provides insights into why it was considered suspicious.
Can I report a false positive?
Yes, you can submit a request through the contact page, and our team will review the flagged transaction.
How secure is my data?
Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow best practices to ensure user privacy and security.
